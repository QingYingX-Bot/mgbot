# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: As of project release or first deployment  
**Applicable to**: All users of XiaoMiGao (hereinafter "the Bot"). The Bot is operated by QingYingX(HInXe) (hereinafter "we" or "the Operator"); this website is for users to read and use.

---

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes the types and purposes of data that the Bot may collect, use and store when running. We process data only as necessary to provide the Bot's features and take reasonable measures to protect your information.

---

## 2. Types of Data Involved

### 2.1 Identity and Account

- **Platform identifiers**: QQ user_id, group_id, Telegram/Discord user IDs when you use the Bot on those platforms.
- **Display information**: Nicknames, group names, avatars and other public info from platforms, used for replies, display and features (e.g. @ mentions, permission checks).

### 2.2 Messages and Interaction

- **Message content**: Text, commands, @ mentions you send, used for command parsing, reply generation and necessary logging (e.g. troubleshooting).
- **Context**: Conversation context and replies involved in fulfilling your commands.

### 2.3 Feature Data (Depending on Your Use)

- **Game/third-party bindings**: e.g. miHoYo, gacha links you voluntarily bind, used to provide you with game data queries.
- **Business data**: Check-in records, gacha records, notes, panel data, etc., used to display or aggregate when you use the relevant features.
- **Configuration and preferences**: Preferences, whitelist/blacklist you or the group set, used for personalization and access control.

---

## 3. Use of Data

- Execute your commands and provide query and entertainment features.
- Account binding, multi-account switching, permission checks (e.g. owner/blacklist).
- Logging and troubleshooting (only as necessary and subject to internal policies).
- Scheduled reminders, notifications and other features you use or have agreed to.

---

## 4. Storage and Retention

- **Storage location**: The above data is stored on the Operator's or the Operator's designated servers and storage, with access control and security measures.
- **Retention**: Data is retained only as long as necessary for the Bot's features or as required by law; non-essential data is periodically purged or anonymized.
- **No sale or sharing with unrelated third parties**: We do not sell your personal information. We share data with third parties only to the minimum extent necessary to provide features (e.g. calling game APIs) or as required by law, in compliance with applicable rules.

---

## 5. Third Parties and External Services

- The Bot may call third-party APIs (e.g. miHoYo, Bilibili) for queries, check-in, binding, etc.
- Calls may pass platform identifiers, cookies/tokens you have bound; see each feature's description and third-party privacy policies.
- We reasonably assess third parties we integrate, but cannot control their data practices; please be aware of the associated risks.

---

## 6. Your Choices and Rights

- **Use implies consent**: Interacting with the Bot or its groups/channels means you acknowledge and accept the collection and use of the above data as necessary.
- **Stopping use**: You may stop using the Bot at any time (e.g. leave the group, unfollow, remove bindings). Doing so reduces further data collection.
- **Deletion and correction**: You can delete bindings or some data via the Bot's commands (e.g. "删除绑定", "删除 cred"); for other personal data deletion or access/correction rights, contact us as set out in "Changes and Contact" below.

---

## 7. Security and Disclaimer

- We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to reduce the risk of disclosure, alteration or loss of information.
- Given the nature of the internet, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You understand and agree that, to the extent permitted by law, the Operator is not liable for data issues caused by force majeure, hacking, or your own actions; except where the law provides otherwise.

---

## 8. Changes and Contact

- This Privacy Policy may be revised with operations or legal requirements; the latest version on this page applies. We will notify you of material changes via this website or other reasonable means.
- For questions, complaints or requests about this policy or your personal information, contact the Operator QingYingX(HInXe) via this website, the project repository or agreed channels (e.g. email: qingyingx@099311.xyz).
